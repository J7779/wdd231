San Francisco Experience

Site Name:

San Francisco Explorer

  • This name highlights the site’s goal of providing a complete and immersive guide for visitors exploring the vibrant city of San Francisco.

Site Purpose:

For the San Francisco Experience

  • This website serves as a go-to resource for both tourists and locals looking to explore San Francisco. It provides insights on top attractions, cultural highlights, iconic landmarks, food recommendations, and practical travel tips, such as public transportation, weather, and must-see neighborhoods.

Scenarios:

  • What are the top tourist attractions to visit in San Francisco?

    This section will cover must-visit landmarks such as the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, Fisherman’s Wharf, and Chinatown, providing details on history, best visiting times, and ticket information.

  • Where can I find the best food in San Francisco?

    Visitors looking for great food experiences will find recommendations on the best places for sourdough bread, seafood at Pier 39, Mission-style burritos, and Michelin-starred restaurants.

  • How do I get around San Francisco efficiently?

    This section will provide insights on public transportation options like BART, Muni, cable cars, and bike rentals, making it easier for visitors to navigate the city.

Color Scheme:

The color scheme for this site includes two main colors:

  • Caribbean Current (hsla(182, 100%, 23%, 1)): Used for headings and accents, representing the cool and modern feel of San Francisco.
  • Floral White (hsla(47, 43%, 96%, 1)): A neutral background to enhance readability and provide a clean aesthetic.

Example CSS HSL:

    /* CSS HSL */
    --black: hsla(0, 0%, 0%, 1);
    --eerie-black: hsla(120, 7%, 11%, 1);
    --caribbean-current: hsla(182, 100%, 23%, 1);
    --light-sea-green: hsla(175, 87%, 40%, 1);

    --floral-white: hsla(47, 43%, 96%, 1);

Typography:

1. Primary Font: Oswald (sans-serif)

  • Usage: Oswald will be used for all headings to create a bold, modern look that complements the city’s dynamic vibe.
  • Applied to:
    • Headings (h1, h2, h3, h4): This ensures clarity and structure for users navigating the content.

2. Secondary Font: Source Sans Pro (sans-serif)

  • Usage: Source Sans Pro will be used for all body text to maintain readability and enhance the user experience.
  • Applied to:
    • Body Text (p): All descriptions, travel tips, and general content will use this font for a comfortable reading experience.
    • Links and Buttons: Ensures visual consistency across interactive elements.
